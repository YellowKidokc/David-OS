## Framework

Given the Fruits of the Spirit formalization, treat the nine Fruits not as moral commands but as observable outputs of a coherent system.

**The Fruits vector:**

Love, Joy, Peace, Patience, Kindness, Goodness, Faithfulness, Gentleness, and Self-Control.

**The anti-Fruits** are not a separate ontology. They are negative values along the same dimensions:

Hatred, Despair, Anxiety, Impatience, Cruelty, Corruption, Betrayal, Harshness, and Addiction.

**The formal model:**

Interpretation:
- χ > χ_c means Fruits begin to manifest.
- χ < χ_c means anti-Fruits appear by default.
- χ = χ_c means the system is unstable or undecided.
- Fruits are diagnostic outputs of coherence.
- Anti-Fruits are diagnostic outputs of incoherence.
- Text should not be judged by whether it mentions the Fruits, but by whether it displays their structure.
